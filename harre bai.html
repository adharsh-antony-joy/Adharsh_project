NATURE##


"Nature" refers to the inherent qualities or constitution of something, encompassing the physical world, including all living things, plants, animals, and natural phenomena, as well as the underlying laws and processes of the universe.

  • Nature is the physical world and everything in it (such as plants, animals, mountains, oceans, stars, etc.) that is not made by people.
  • Nature can also refer to the natural world and the way things exist in it, without human intervention.
  • Nature can also refer to the inherent qualities or characteristics of something, such as a person's nature or the nature of a particular situation.
  • Nature can also refer to the natural environment, including the air, water, and land, and the ecosystems that support life on Earth.
      • Nature can also refer to the natural world as a whole, including all living and non-living things, and the interactions between them.
